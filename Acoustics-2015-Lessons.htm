Applied Acoustics Course - A.Y. 2015/2016

Calendar with link to PPT presentations, AVI audio-video recordings, notes and in-class tests

  • This page is the course calendar, the link to didactic material (Powerpoint slides, audio-video recording of the lessons, notes taken by student of previous year), and contains links to in-class tests, to their results and, at the bottom of the page, to the final results of in-class activity.

  • It must be remembered that this is a "flipped classroom" course, and the main goal of the in-class tests is NOT to pass the exam directly!

  • The goal is to learn the technical topics of Applied Acoustics: the possibility that these in-class tests provide the partial or total exemption from the final written tests is just a possibility which will be reached by a small number of excellent students.

  • For all the others (the majority), the in-class test will NOT provide exemption from the exam tests, but it is expected that students who follow the course in its entirety and take part to the in-class tests will reach enough confidence in the matter, so that they will pass the final exam easily.
