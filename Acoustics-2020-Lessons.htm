  • This page is the course calendar, the link to didactic material (Powerpoint slides, audio-video recording of the lessons and notes, of previous years), and contains links to in-class tests, to their results and to the final results of in-class activity.

  • On each date the students should connect to the MS Team linked above, after having watched the corresponding video lessons and having read the notes. Having downloaded also the Powerpoint slides makes it easy to follow the lessons.

  • On each day, during the first hour (08-09) a Q&A chat session is held, with no audio-video. During the second hour (09-10) an interactive seminar occurs, where an assistant or the students themselves solve some numerical problems related to the topic of the day or make experiments recording and processing sounds with different type of software.

  • In some cases a student will have to explain a concept to the colleague.

This spreadsheet contains the weighted score for the whole set of 5 in-class tests, scaled to 30. Who reached 18/30 or above can do the oral exam directly. In any case all students can try to improve the score obtained with the in-class tests by performing the written exam test. The better of the two scores will be taken into account.
