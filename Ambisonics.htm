Ambisonics Pages on pcfarina

Ambisonics is a multichannel audio technology, allowing for encoding, recording and reproduction of audio signals containing complete spatial information

The following web pages are hosted on pcfarina.eng.unipr.it, dealing with various aspects of Ambisonics technology:

  • Conversion between UHJ and B-format (UHJ decoding)
  • Conversion between B-format and UHJ (UHJ encoding)
  • Conversion between A-format and B-format (home-made Soundfield microphone)
  • Performance analysis of three Ambisonics decoders over an ITU 5.1 surround sound system.
  • Bass Management new freeware, open-source VST plugin
  • An example B-format recording, and stereo downmix for MP3 delivery
  • The new Casa del Suono research facility is open!
  • List of papers related to the Ambisonics technology authored by Angelo Farina
