Pre-requisites

This course assumes that students have already acquired some basic knowledge during their three-years degree, and in particular:

  • heat dispersion through the building envelope
  • thermophysical properties of moist air
  • fundamentals of acoustics
  • fundamentals of lighting technology

If necessary, students who are not already in possession of this basic knowledge can integrate their cultural background by using the teaching material (slides, handouts, video recording of the lessons) taken from the web page of the Environmental Technical Physics course held over the past years:

  • Fisica Tecnica Ambientale (A/Y 2019-20)

Exam Project (Laboratory) - 2nd Semester
The activity carried out in the first part of the course, before Easter, is aimed at developing a project, to be carried out during the second part (after Easter 2022), which is assigned to groups of 2 students.
Students are entrusted with the drafting of a urban planning project related in an urban context with high population density, for which they will have to produce the technical documentation required by the current regulatory framework, and more specifically the following two documents:

  • acoustics climate analysis (including assessment of noise sources, if present) - Here you find the current noise maps of Eindhoven
  • design of the new public lighting system - Here you find the online Philips catalog of luminaires

At each lesson, some students present the progress of their project and the whole class interacts providing suggestions and criticisms. 5 interactive labs of 4h each are planned, for a total of 20 hours.
