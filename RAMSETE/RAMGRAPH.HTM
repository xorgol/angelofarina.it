Ramsete Graph  

Ramsete Graph is the first post processor: it reads the file produced by the Tracer (which is an ASCII, self documented file), and visulazises the impulse responses, the Schroeder's plots, the SPL spectra and the reverberation time's spectra. All these data can also be viewed in tabular forms, and exported to other programs (spreadsheets, etc.).

The data are available in 10 octave bands plus A and LIN weightings. It is possible to view the results for each receiver, or the averaged (room) response.
