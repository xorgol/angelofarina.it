Ramsete View 

Ramsete View is the second post processor: it displays the room's geometry both in plan view and in perspective view, and allows the creation of colour maps of the most used acoustic parameters, as it is shown in the following picture.

Making use of the Contour Line capabilities of the external program Surfer (TM), Ramsete View is also capable of displaying the data as contour plots.

Other features include the calculation of RASTI and STI, taking into account also the background noise, and the capability to export all the results through the clipboard or through self documented ASCII files.

Ramsete View includes also a visual rendering subrogram, which anyway cannot be compared with the rendering capabilities of Autodesk's AutoCAD or 3DStudio.
